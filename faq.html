Are nail salons safe during COVID?

Are you looking for a safe and hygienic nail salon in Ottawa? We've been in business for over 15 years and follow all Provincial and Federal regulations. What are dipping powder nails?

Tired of the problems with traditional acrylic and gel nails? Dip powder is a brand new nail enhancement, offering some of the most beautiful and longest-lasting nail enhancements available. Using only natural products that strengthen your nails rather than damage them—and providing up to four weeks of chip-free, gorgeous color—this product is sure to become an instant favorite!

What is Shellac?

Shellac is a gel polish that cures really fast, and last really long. It's applied just like nail polish and cured under a UV/LED light. Shellac is a combination of a natural ingredient and polymer resin, which gives it its unique combination of strength, durability, flexibility, and shine.
