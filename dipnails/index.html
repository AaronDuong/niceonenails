NuGenesis Nails dipping powder technology offers exceptionally vibrant colors and neutral favorites for all seasons and occasions. Dip powder can stay on natural nails for up to 2-4 weeks. Keep in mind nail growth differs from person to person due to factors such as age, gender, health, weather, and hygiene. Many nail professionals and their customers have experienced and seen the results of stronger natural nails after using the dip powder process. The results are typically visible immediately after the first application and very noticeable among those who have switched to dip powder completely.This is a great alternative for those who are allergic to various ingredients in Gel and Acrylic Nails.
